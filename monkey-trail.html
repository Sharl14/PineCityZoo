MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a fascinating exhibit that showcases various species of monkeys in their naturalistic habitats. Visitors can observe these intelligent primates as they swing, climb, and interact with each other.

The trail features a variety of monkey species, each with unique behaviors and characteristics. From playful capuchins to agile spider monkeys, the exhibit provides an opportunity to learn about the diverse world of monkeys and their importance in the ecosystem.

The Monkey Trail is designed to educate visitors about the conservation of primates and their habitats. It highlights the challenges faced by these animals in the wild and the efforts being made to protect them. The exhibit also emphasizes the importance of preserving biodiversity and the role of zoos in conservation efforts.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find it at the main square, next to the amphitheater.
