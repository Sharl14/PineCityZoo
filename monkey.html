ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY

Monkeys are highly intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures. They are found in various habitats, including forests, savannas, and mountains.

Monkeys are known for their dexterity and ability to use tools. They communicate using vocalizations, facial expressions, and body language.

Monkeys are social animals that live in groups called troops. They have complex social hierarchies and engage in grooming behaviors to strengthen bonds within the group.
