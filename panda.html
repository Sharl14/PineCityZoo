PANDAS

Pandas are large, bear-like mammals native to China, known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily feed on bamboo, although they are technically omnivores.

Pandas are solitary animals, spending most of their time foraging for food and resting. They have a slow reproductive rate, which has contributed to their status as a vulnerable species.

Pandas are known for their playful behavior, often engaging in climbing and rolling around in their natural habitat.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find them at pen E22, right next to the amphitheatre.
