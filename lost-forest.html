LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a magical place filled with lush greenery and exotic plants. It is home to various species of animals, including the elusive red panda and the majestic snow leopard.

Visitors can explore the forest through well-marked trails, where they can observe the unique flora and fauna. The forest is also a sanctuary for endangered species, and efforts are made to preserve its natural beauty and biodiversity.

The Lost Forest is not just a place for wildlife; it also offers educational programs and guided tours to raise awareness about conservation efforts. Visitors can learn about the importance of protecting natural habitats and the role of zoos in wildlife conservation.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

You will find it at the main square, next to the amphitheater.
